Rules

      Welcome to Excalibur Community, where every member is a noble knight or fair maiden committed to upholding the highest standards of honor and integrity.
      To maintain the sanctity of our realm and ensure a harmonious experience for all, we have crafted the following code of conduct:
  1. Chivalrous Conduct: Treat every member with the utmost respect and kindness. Conduct yourself as a true knight or maiden, embodying the virtues of courtesy, generosity, and compassion in all interactions.
  2. Nobility of Intent: Approach each connection with sincerity and honesty. Do not deceive or mislead others, for trust is the cornerstone of every meaningful relationship.
  3. Honor the Boundaries: Respect the boundaries and preferences of your fellow members. Do not engage in harassment, discrimination, or any form of disrespectful behavior. Remember, consent is paramount.
  4. Shield of Privacy: Safeguard the personal information of yourself and others. Refrain from sharing sensitive details or engaging in any activities that compromise the privacy and security of our community.
  5. Sword of Justice: Report any misconduct or violations of our community guidelines promptly. We rely on the vigilance of our members to maintain a safe and welcoming environment for all.
  6. Quest for Connection: Embark on your journey with an open heart and a willingness to explore. Be receptive to different perspectives and embrace the diversity of experiences that our community has to offer.

    7. By adhering to these noble principles, we can ensure that Excalibur Community remains a beacon of hope and companionship for all who seek it. Together, let us forge bonds that withstand the test of time and stand as a testament to the power of love and camaraderie.
